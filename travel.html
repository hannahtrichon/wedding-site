Airport Information

For those traveling from further away for the occasion, we recommend the following airports:

PHL — Philadelphia International
approximately 60 minutes driving to the venue

EWR — Newark Liberty International
approximately 70 minutes driving to the venue

LGA — LaGuardia International
Approximately 100 minutes driving to the venue

JFK — John F Kennedy International
Approximately 120 minutes driving to the venue

Regional Transportation Information

Travel from New York City / Newark

NJ Transit
If you are coming from New York City or flying into the New York area airports (EWR, LGA, JFK), NJ Transit is available from New York Penn Station and Newark Penn Station. If you are taking NJ Transit, you can disembark at Hamilton Train Station.

Travel from Philadelphia

If you are flying into Philadelphia, the Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) train is available from PHL. If you are taking SEPTA, you can disembark Doylestown Train Station.

Rideshare / Car Rentals

Rideshare apps (e.g., Uber, Lyft) are widely available in the area.

Car rentals are also a great option for those interested in exploring the local area.
