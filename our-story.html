Our Story

Hannah & Cole

How It Started

We met on our first day at Boston University — at a hot dog cart line during the commencement afterparty. What started as a simple “can I cut in line with you?” turned into a friendship that would grow for years before becoming something more.

The Engagement

Family and friends mean everything to us, so when Cole proposed, he made sure the celebration included the people Hannah loves most. Despite a little rain and a near restaurant detour, the surprise party went off without a hitch — and we’ve been planning ever since.

The Wedding

Our wedding weekend is about bringing together the people who shaped us, supported us, and loved us through every phase of life. We are endlessly grateful to celebrate with you.

With love, Hannah & Cole
